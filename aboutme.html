Hey There!

I'm Rajat Rajendra Prabhu and I've been developing games for the past 3 years as a Gameplay Programmer/Systems Designer. I work towards making players enjoy their gameplay experience and make games more fun and engaging to play!

Ever since video-games became a part of my life, I've embarked on several journeys through their worlds inspiring me to create similar experiences for others. Through this journey, I've gained a deeper understanding of how fun game mechanics can captivate players and create memorable experiences.

Alongside creating and playing video-games, I enjoy playing football and cooking dishes from a variety of cuisines. These allow me to unwind and engage in physical activity, and tap into my creativity in a different way, much like developing games!
